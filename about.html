What is Fika?

Fika is a Swedish inspired cafe based in Woolton, Liverpool. Fika is swedish custom where people take time out of their day to relax, eat cake, have drinks and spend time with others, usually in a relaxing environment.

Fika Liverpool has the same concept. Come and meet the Fika team and enjoy some of the delicious snacks and drinks on offer.
